Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

DeepChecks Trading is a digital subscription service delivered through secure TradingView invite-only access. This policy explains when refunds are available, so expectations are clear before you subscribe.

1. Refund Window

If you are not satisfied with your subscription, you may request a full refund within 3 days of your original purchase, provided your invite-only access has not yet been substantially used. This gives new subscribers a fair opportunity to confirm the indicators are activated correctly and suit their setup before the window closes.

2. Always Eligible for a Refund

Regardless of the window above, the following are always eligible for a refund:

  • Duplicate or accidental double payment for the same plan.
  • Technical delivery failure — where invite-only access could not be granted within a reasonable time despite a correct TradingView username and follow-up with our support team.
  • Any case where applicable consumer protection law entitles you to a refund.

3. Renewals

For Quarterly and Yearly plans, you can cancel auto-renewal at any time from before your next billing date to avoid being charged again — access simply continues until the end of the period you already paid for. Once a renewal payment has been successfully charged and the new period's access has started, that renewal charge is treated the same as a new purchase under Section 1 above (a 3-day window applies from the renewal date).

4. What Isn't Covered

Because our indicators are analytical tools only and do not guarantee any trading outcome, dissatisfaction with trading results is not, on its own, grounds for a refund. Refund requests made after the window in Section 1, for reasons unrelated to a technical delivery failure, are evaluated at our discretion on a case-by-case basis — we'd rather look into a fair resolution with you than lose you to a payment dispute, so reach out even if you're just unsure.

5. Incorrect TradingView Username

Please double-check your TradingView username when subscribing. Activation delays caused by an incorrect or misspelled username are corrected as soon as you provide the right one, and do not by themselves extend or restart the refund window in Section 1.

6. How to Request a Refund

Email deepchecks.support@gmail.com with your payment confirmation and TradingView username. We aim to review and respond to every request as quickly as possible during support hours.
